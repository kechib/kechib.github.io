I began my interest in programming self-teaching myself the basics of Ruby. After completing free intro courses and challenges, I found myself engrossed in the learning process. My goal in joining the program was to challenge myself and create websites that I’m proud of. Since being in the program, I believe that some of my strengths are the ability to rise to a challenge and develop solutions to problems and situations.

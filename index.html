About Me!

I’m a junior software engineer who is currently at the end of a 3 month Software Engineering Immersive bootcamp program with General Assembly. I began my interest in programming self teaching myself the basics of Ruby. After completing free intro courses and challenges I found myself engrossed in the learning process. My goal in joining the program was to challenge myself and create websites that I’m proud of. Since being in the program, I believe that some of my strengths are the ability to rise to a challenge, and develop solutions to problems and situations. I am driven by self growth and taking something I am given and creating a product or solution with my personal flare. My overall goal as a newcomer in the software engineering field is to learn to simplify solutions to technology’s complex problems, and use the knowledge I am growing to possess and learned, to keep improving the quality and ease of work for user interactions and software. I hope to obtain a position that will allow me to utilize and apply my experiences to achieve growth and experience in this field.
